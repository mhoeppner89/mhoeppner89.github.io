Behavioral economics · risk · decision theory

Research on decisions under risk and uncertainty.

I am a postdoctoral researcher in behavioral economics at Brandenburg University of Technology Cottbus-Senftenberg. My work studies how people and adaptive systems choose when outcomes are noisy, stakes are uneven, and institutional environments create thresholds or pressure.
